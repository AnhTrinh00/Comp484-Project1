About This Project

Project Allies is a plain HTML + CSS site that introduces core CS concepts with short explanations and media. It avoids heavy frameworks and keeps the design black and white.

Goals

  • Be simple and readable.
  • Demonstrate media tags: <video>, <audio>, <embed>.
  • Use semantic tags: <abbr>, <details>, <cite>.
  • Follow the “4 HTML + 1 CSS” structure.

How It’s Built

FilePurpose
index.htmlWelcome page and quick navigation.
media.htmlVideo and audio demonstrations.
examples.htmlInteractive details and an embedded PDF.
about.htmlProject rationale, attributions, and FAQ.
styles.cssMinimal black-and-white styling.

FAQ

Why black and white?

To keep focus on content, minimize distractions, and meet project constraints.

Can I add more topics?

Yes—add more <details> blocks in examples.html and update the cheat sheet.

Where are the media files stored?

In the assets/ folder (MP4, MP3/OGG, and PDF).
