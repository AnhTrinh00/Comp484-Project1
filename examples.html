What is an XOR operation?

XOR outputs 1 only when the inputs differ (01 or 10). It’s widely used in parity checks and cryptography primitives.

What’s the difference between RAM and Storage?

RAM is volatile working memory; storage (like SSD) persists data across power cycles.
